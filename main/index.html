LearnSync is a Learning Management System (LMS) designed to streamline the management of courses, students, and learning resources. It provides an organized platform where instructors can upload materials, manage student progress, and track performance, while learners can easily access lessons, submit tasks, and monitor their achievements all in one synchronized system.
